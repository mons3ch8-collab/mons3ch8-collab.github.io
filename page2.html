What is Chips + Malt?

Chip+Malt is a contemporary fish and chip shop on historic Main St. in Newmarket, Ontario. Inspired by a shared love for traditional British fish and chips and a dedication to using fresh, ethically sourced ingredients, the restaurant was built as a true labour of love, focusing on quality, craftsmanship, and authenticity.
